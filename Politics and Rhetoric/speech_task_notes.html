I said I would be tough on crime, tough on the causes on crime. And this had led to interventions in communities making a real difference, improving the quality of life for people, reducing crime, reducing disorder, reducing fear. returning to the british people the trust they placed in me.


I said I would seek out poverty. My government has for the last decade delivered policy targeting poverty, lifting children out of deprivation, paying a decent minimum wage, ending degrading means testing. we have raised the general level of wealth and prosperity of the people of this nation, repaying the trust they placed in me


my leadership of this government has ensured we delivered on our commitments. Earning again and again, for second and then a historic third term, the trust of the british people.
